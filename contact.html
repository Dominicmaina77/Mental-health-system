Frequently Asked Questions ‚ùì

Quick answers to common questions

Is SootheSpace really free? +

Yes! SootheSpace is completely free to use. We believe mental health support should be accessible to everyone. We never charge for our core features and we don't show ads.

How do you protect my privacy? +

Your privacy is our top priority. All data is encrypted, we don't sell your information, and your journal entries are private by default. You can export or delete your data anytime.

Is this a replacement for therapy? +

No. SootheSpace is designed as a self-help tool for emotional tracking and wellness. It's not a replacement for professional mental health care. We always encourage seeking professional help when needed.

What age group is SootheSpace for? +

We're designed for youth and young adults (ages 13-25), but anyone can benefit from our tools. Our content and features are tailored to be youth-friendly and accessible.

Can I use SootheSpace on my phone? +

Yes! SootheSpace works on all devices - phones, tablets, and computers. We're also working on mobile apps for even better mobile experience.
